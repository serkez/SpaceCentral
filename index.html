Perseverance Mars Rover

On July 30, 2020, at 7:50 a.m. EDT, the Perseverance rover began its journey to the Red Planet. Persaverance will seek signs of ancient life on Mars as well as collect rock and soil samples to send back to Earth. A helicopter named Ingenuity was sent with the rover. Ingenuity will mark the first powered flight on another planet. Persaverance and Ingenuity are set to arrive on Mars on February 18, 2021.

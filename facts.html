Cool Facts


It would take nine years to walk to the moon

It rains diamonds on Jupiter and Saturn

People on the International Space Station age just a tiny bit slower than people on Earth

Neutron stars which form after a massive star dies are so dense that a teaspoon of neutron star material would weigh more than a billion tons

Mercury's temperature changes from -280° F on its night side to 800° F during the day

Our solar system is aproximatly 165 quadrillion miles away from the center of the milky way galaxy

The universe has no center

A day on Venus is longer than a year on venus. A day on Venus lasts 243 Earth days A year on Venus lasts only 225 Earth days

99.86% of our solar system's mass is in the Sun

Uranus is tilted on its side

Io, one of Jupiter's moons, has active volcanoes

Nuclear pasta, a material that is theorized to exist in the crust of nuetron stars, is the strongest material in the universe. (nuclear pasta is 10 billion times harder to break than steel)

The international Space Station orbits Earth every 90 minutes

There is microgravity on the International Space Station. That means that things appear to be weightless but they really float because they are falling at the same rate as the space station

Space is almost completely silent

Cool Facts


There is microgravity on the International Space Station. That means that things appear to be weightless but they actually float because they are falling at the same rate as the space station

People on the International Space Station age just a tiny bit slower than people on Earth

Neutron stars which form after a massive star dies are so dense that a teaspoon of neutron star material would weigh more than a billion tons.

Nuclear pasta, a material that is theorized to exist in the crust of nuetron stars, is the strongest material in the universe. (nuclear pasta is 10 billion times harder to break than steel)

Our solar system is aproximatly 165 quadrillion miles away from the center of the milky way galaxy

The universe has no center

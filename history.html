A (not so) BRIEF HISTORY OF SPACE EXPLORATION



THE TELESCOPE

Humans have been observing the night sky for as long as we existed. In 1608 a Dutch eyeglass became the first person to apply for a patent for a telescope that magnified objects three times. About a year later, Galileo Galilei after hearing about the telescope, created his own that could magnify objects 20 times. He was the first to use the telescope to onserve the sky discovering Saturn's rings and four of Jupiter's moons. In 1633 Galileo was sentanced to house arrest after claiming that Earth moved around the Sun.

In 1668 Isaac Newton built a reflecting telescope which used mirrors instead of lenses. The development of the radio allowed scientist to study not only light from space, but also other electromagnetic radiation.
